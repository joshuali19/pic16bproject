How to Find a Playlist ID on Spotify

  1. Open Spotify and go to the playlist that you want to find the ID for.
  2. Click on the three dots that appear next to the playlist name.
  3. A drop-down menu will appear. Click on the "Share" option.
  4. A new window will appear with several sharing options. Click on the "Copy Link" button.
  5. Paste the link you just copied into a text editor or note-taking app.
  6. Look for the section of the link that starts with "/playlist/". The playlist ID is the combination of numbers and letters that follow this section, up to the next forward slash "/" or the question mark "?", if it exists.
  7. If the link has a question mark "?", make sure to only copy the characters of the playlist ID before the question mark, as the additional parameters are not part of the playlist ID.
  8. Once you've identified the correct playlist ID, you can use it to share your playlist with others, or to use it in third-party applications that require a playlist ID.
